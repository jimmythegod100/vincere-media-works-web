FAQ

Common questions

How do I start a project?

Call or text (209) 315-5702, or send the form below with your project type and budget. We reply within 24–48 hours with next steps and a quote.

What’s included in the Website Launch Package?

A custom mobile-first page with click-to-call, services, hours/map, basic local SEO, and visitor analytics. From $897 — half to start, half when we go live. Typical turnaround is about one week after we have your photos and deposit.

Do you offer SEO, analytics, and maintenance?

Yes. Launch packages include foundational SEO and analytics. Ongoing maintenance (updates, backups, uptime monitoring) is available as an add-on so your site stays healthy after go-live.

What’s your typical turnaround time?

Launch websites: about 5–10 business days. Starter creative: 3–5 days. Larger brand/video packages: 1–4 weeks depending on scope. Rush delivery available for an additional fee.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Every package includes at least one revision round. Additional revisions can be added. We want you satisfied before final delivery.

Do you only work in the Central Valley?

We’re based in the Central Valley and love working with local shops — and we also take remote clients nationwide via phone, email, and cloud file sharing.
