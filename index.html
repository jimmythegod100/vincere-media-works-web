FAQ

Common questions

What exactly do you do?

We design and build websites for businesses — with local SEO setup, visitor analytics, click-to-call, and optional maintenance. Logo design is available as an add-on when you need it for the site.

What’s included in the Website Launch Package?

A custom mobile-first site with your services, hours/map, click-to-call, local SEO foundations, and analytics connected. From $897 — half to start, half when we go live. Typical timeline is about one week after we have your photos and deposit.

Do you only make websites in one style or color?

No. Your site is designed for your business — colors, layout, and tone matched to you. Our own brand uses a bold look on this page; that’s our company style, not a template we force onto clients.

What is SEO and analytics in plain English?

SEO is how we structure your site so Google can understand and show it when people search. Analytics shows how many people visit, what they look at, and whether they tap Call or submit a form.

Do you maintain the site after launch?

Yes — optional care plans cover updates, backups, uptime monitoring, and small content changes so the site stays secure and current.
